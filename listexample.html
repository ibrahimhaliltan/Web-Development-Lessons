Ingredients

For the meat;

  • Half a kilo of ground beef
  • A pich of parsley
  • A teaspoon of black pepper
  • A tablespoon of salt
  • A teaspoon of hot pepper

For the sauce;

  • Three tablespoons of mayonnaise
  • Four tablespoons of ketchup
  • Two tablespoons of mustard
  • Half chopped onion
  • Chopped pickled cucumber
  • A tablespoon of pickle juice
  • A teaspoon of honey

Instructions

  1. We knead the minced meat for fifteen minutes, adding the spices in the quantities we prepared.
  2. We form the minced meat kneaded with spices into a ball in the palm of our hands. Each ball should weigh approximately 70 grams.
  3. Let the balls you prepared rest in the fridge for 1 hour before cooking.
  4. Let's quickly mix the ingredients we prepared for the sauce for five minutes, we need to bring this sauce to a consistency that can be spread on the bread.
  5. Before cooking, heat the pan with a tablespoon of oil.
  6. Let's place the minced meat balls that we have kept waiting on the heated pan, crush them with a spatula and shape them into a shape to put between bread.
  7. Let's turn the cooked meatballs for five minutes, front and back, and put them on a plate.
  8. Let's heat the front and back of the bread in an oiled pan for a few minutes and spread the sauce into the bread.
  9. Let's put the cooked meat between the bread we sauced.
  10. Enjoy your meal !
